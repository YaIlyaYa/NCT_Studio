About NCT Studio

Independent CGI & animation team

NCT Studio is an independent CGI and animation team producing original stylized 3D series for YouTube audiences worldwide. The studio focuses on cinematic storytelling, creature animation, worldbuilding and high-quality visual production.

In addition to our original series, we provide custom client work including 3D modeling, animation, texturing, and full-cycle development of bosses and mobs from concept to completion.
